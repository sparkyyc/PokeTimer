Welcome Pokemon trainer! Each time you use this timer you will be able to go on an adventure to collect a new pokemon or evolve a pokemon you've already collected. You can choose the egg, to hatch a new pokemon, or a pokemon in your pokedex that you found on a previous adventure as your adventure buddy. Each pokemon has a set amount of time they need to evolve. At the end of each timer your new pokemon, evolved pokemon, or time left to evolve will be revealed. You can click on the ? in the top right corner for more info and a reminder of what to do.
